Created in Photoshop CS2.

A simple painting of a chorus of the colors of spring using my custom brushes. Spring puts joy in my heart and song on my lips.

I kept this simple because I notice that many flowers are rather simple in the design, some delicate, some strong, but all with amazing beauty and more colors than a painters palette can hold.

My emphasis in this painting is the color.
